Phishing email investigation and malicious URL analysis.

A phishing email analysis was conducted to identify malicious indicators commonly associated with social engineering attacks. The investigation involved analysing suspicious sender information, phishing characteristics, embedded links, and malicious URL reputation using VirusTotal.
